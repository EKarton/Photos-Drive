Photos Drive

View your photos better

About
This is a front-facing web application that connects to your Photos Drive instance. You can securely view your photos and videos on any web browser.
Secure
All actions in this web application can only be read. To modify the contents of your Photos Drive, you will have to use the CLI. All users must be authenticated via OAuth2.
Easy to use
You can view your photos in many ways - list view, or map view within an albums or across all albums. You can even search through your photos via Gemini.

Intelligent Search

Search for photos using Gemini and Agentic Search

View your photos better

View all of your photos or photos inside an album on a map

Embedded Viewer

View, share, download images and videos in high resolution

Mobile Friendly

Easily access your images and videos from your phone
